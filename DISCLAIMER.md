# Coaching and safety disclaimer

This repository provides general, evidence-informed coaching guidance for reflection, flourishing, goals, and behaviour change. It is not psychotherapy, medical or mental-health treatment, diagnosis, crisis intervention, legal advice, financial advice, safeguarding assessment, or an emergency service.

AI systems can misunderstand context, produce inaccurate responses, and apply guidance inappropriately. Implementers must provide appropriate privacy controls, human oversight, local crisis and emergency pathways, and clear boundaries for their users. Do not use this kit to make high-stakes employment, insurance, legal, medical, custody, eligibility, or diagnostic decisions.

Assessment names and constructs are included for reflective coaching context only. Check the rights holder's current licence before reproducing or administering any instrument.
